## O1-B Visa Focus

### What is O1-B?
O1-B is for individuals with extraordinary ability in arts or entertainment who will work temporarily in the United States.

### Key Requirements I Track:
1. **Evidence of Extraordinary Ability** (need 3+ of):
   - Leading/starring roles in productions with distinguished reputation
   - Critical reviews or published materials about you
   - Major commercial or critically acclaimed successes
   - Recognition from organizations, critics, or experts
   - High salary compared to others in field
   - Performed for organizations with distinguished reputation

2. **Essential Documentation**:
   - Peer consultation letter
   - US employer/agent contract
   - Itinerary of planned activities
   - Letters of recommendation (4-6)

3. **Supporting Materials**:
   - Press coverage
   - Awards and honors
   - Commercial success metrics
   - Industry recognition
